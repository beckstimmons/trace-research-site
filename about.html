About Trace Research

Follow the record. Find what matters.

Trace Research is an independent intelligence firm specializing in due diligence, litigation support, and complex fact-finding.

Trace Research: Our mission is in the name.

At Trace Research, we supplement the research needs of your organization, but we do it differently than others. We deliver actionable intelligence that uncovers new information and, crucially, we show your team exactly how we found it. We trace back our methodologies to equip your team with the tools necessary to keep digging.

We employ an open-source, document-centered methodology informed by investigative journalism, business intelligence, legal research, and data analysis. Our work is designed to uncover relevant facts, assess risk, map relationships, and turn fragmented information into clear findings.

Trace is built for matters where accuracy, discretion, and sourcing matter. We don't just deliver answers, we show how those answers were found, allowing clients to understand the evidence behind every conclusion.
